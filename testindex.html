Services

Tax Preparation

We prepare federal and state income tax returns for individuals, entrepreneurs, creators, and small businesses, with a focus on accurate reporting, proper treatment of income and deductions, and timely filing.

Real Estate Compliance

We provide tax preparation and compliance services for real estate investors and partnerships, with a focus on single-tenant commercial properties, shopping centers and office buildings up to 100,000 square feet, and residential rental properties.

Expat & International Tax

We assist U.S. citizens abroad and foreign entities with U.S. tax obligations, including cross-border reporting, FBAR, Forms 5471 and 5472, foreign earned income exclusion, foreign tax credits, and treaty considerations.

State & Local Tax Services

We help businesses and individuals navigate multistate tax issues, including nexus analysis, voluntary disclosure agreements, audit support, remote work considerations, and non-resident filings.

Who We Are

We are a modern CPA practice based in South Florida, serving individuals, freelancers, small business owners, and U.S. expats worldwide. With over 15 years of experience, we focus on accurate, responsive, and practical tax solutions.
