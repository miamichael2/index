Our Services

Tax compliance and accounting services for individuals, small businesses, real estate investors, and U.S. clients abroad.

Tax Preparation

We prepare federal and state tax returns for real estate investors, entrepreneurs, and small businesses. Our work focuses on accurate reporting, proper treatment of income and deductions, and meeting all filing requirements on time. Return pricing begins at $400 for basic individual returns and $1,050 for low-activity business returns.

Real Estate

We provide tax preparation and compliance services for real estate investors, with a focus on single-tenant commercial properties, shopping centers and office buildings up to 100,000 square feet, and residential rental properties. We'll handle your ongoing compliance as your portfolio grows.

Expat & International Tax

We help U.S. citizens abroad and foreign entities with U.S. tax obligations. Our clients trust us to handle all aspects of their cross-border U.S. tax compliance; FBAR, 5471, 5472, E&P studies, foreign earned income exclusion (Form 2555), foreign tax credits, residency considerations, and tax treaty positions.

State & Local Tax Services

We help businesses and individuals navigate multistate tax issues across multiple jurisdictions. This includes nexus analysis, Voluntary Disclosure Agreements (VDA), audit support, remote work considerations, non-resident credits, and related state and local compliance matters.

Who We Are

We are a modern CPA practice based in South Florida, serving individuals, freelancers, small business owners, and U.S. expats worldwide. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in accurate, responsive, and practical tax solutions.
