Our Services

Tax Preparation & Compliance

We prepare federal and state tax returns for real estate investors, entrepreneurs, and small businesses. Our real estate practice focuses on single-tenant commercial properties, shopping centers and office buildings up to 100,000 sq ft, and residential properties. Return pricing begins at $400 for basic individual returns, $1,050 for low-activity business returns.

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Full-service bookkeeping using QuickBooks and Xero. We reconcile accounts, categorize expenses, and provide clean books and reports on a monthly or quarterly basis. Service packages begin at $300 per month for entrepreneurs.

Expat & International Tax

We specialize in helping U.S. citizens abroad and foreign entities with U.S. obligations. We guide you through tax compliance, FBAR filings, and foreign tax credit optimization, helping you stay compliant while reducing your cross-border tax burden.

State & Local Tax Services

We help businesses and individuals navigate complex state and local tax rules across multiple jurisdictions. From sales tax and income tax nexus, to Voluntary Disclosure Agreements (VDA) and audits, we guide you through every step to stay compliant and avoid costly surprises.

Who We Are

We are a modern CPA practice based in South Florida, serving individuals, freelancers, small business owners, and U.S. expats worldwide. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in accurate, responsive, and practical tax solutions. Our principal, Michael Abbott, is a CPA licensed in the State of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from the University at Buffalo and a Master of Science in Taxation from the University of Miami.
