Our Services

  • Tax Preparation & Compliance for Small Businesses and Individuals

    We prepare and file federal and state tax returns for individuals, freelancers, sole proprietors, and small businesses. Our compliance services are tailored to your situation, including estimated tax payments, 1099 reporting, and entity-specific strategies. We ensure your filings are accurate, timely, and meet IRS and state regulations. Individual return pricing begins at $750.

  • Bookkeeping & Accounting

    Whether you're just starting out or growing fast, we offer full-service bookkeeping using platforms like QuickBooks Online and Xero. We reconcile accounts, handle expense categorization, generate financial reports, and collaborate with you or your team on a monthly or quarterly basis. Our bookkeeping ensures your books are clean, organized, and tax-ready.

  • Expat and International Tax Services

    We serve U.S. citizens living abroad and foreign businesses or individuals with U.S. tax obligations. Our services include foreign earned income exclusions, FBAR and FATCA compliance, treaty planning, and support for dual-resident taxpayers. We simplify the complexities of cross-border reporting and help minimize international tax exposure.
