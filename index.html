Our Services

Tax Preparation & Compliance

We prepare federal and state tax returns for individuals, freelancers, and small businesses. We handle estimated taxes, 1099s, and entity-specific strategies. Individual return pricing begins at $750.

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Full-service bookkeeping using QuickBooks and Xero. We reconcile accounts, categorize expenses, and provide clean books and reports on a monthly or quarterly basis. Service packages begin at $300 per month for entrepreneurs.

Expat & International Tax

We specialize in helping U.S. citizens abroad and foreign entities with U.S. obligations. We guide you through tax compliance, FBAR filings, and nexus issues, helping you stay compliant while reducing your cross-border tax burden.

Who We Are

We are a modern CPA practice based in South Florida, serving individuals, freelancers, small business owners, and U.S. expats worldwide. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in accurate, responsive, and practical tax solutions. Our principal, Michael Abbott, is a CPA licensed in the State of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from the University at Buffalo and a Master of Science in Taxation from the University of Miami.
