Tax Preparation

We prepare federal and state tax returns for real estate investors, entrepreneurs, influencers, creators, and small businesses. Our work focuses on accurate reporting, proper treatment of income and deductions, and meeting all filing requirements on time.

Pricing begins at $400 for individual returns and $1,050 for low-activity business returns.

Real Estate Compliance

We provide tax preparation and compliance for real estate investors, including single-tenant commercial properties, shopping centers and office buildings up to 100,000 square feet, and residential rental properties.

Expat & International Tax

We assist U.S. citizens abroad and foreign entities with U.S. tax obligations, including cross-border reporting, FBAR, Forms 5471 and 5472, foreign earned income exclusion, foreign tax credits, and treaty considerations.

State & Local Tax Services

We help businesses and individuals navigate multistate tax issues, including nexus analysis, voluntary disclosure agreements, audit support, remote work considerations, and non-resident filings.

Who We Are

We are a modern CPA practice based in South Florida, serving individuals, freelancers, small business owners, and U.S. expats worldwide. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in accurate, responsive, and practical tax solutions.
